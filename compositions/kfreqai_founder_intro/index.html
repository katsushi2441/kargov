Hi, I'm Atsushi Kojima, founder of Exbridge in Japan.
Most AI trading systems focus only on prediction.
I wanted a system that reviews mistakes and improves through evidence.
Kurage combines FreqAI and LightGBM with AI feedback loops.
Market analysis, risk control, trade review, and strategy improvement.
Every change is backtested. A human makes the final decision.
It supports both paper trading and live trading.
We publish performance, losses, incidents, and lessons through a public dashboard and blog.
I launched it to make AI trading more transparent, testable, and continuously improvable.
