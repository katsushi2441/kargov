# Kurage FreqAI Trade Founder Intro

1. Hi, I'm Atsushi Kojima, founder of Exbridge in Japan.
2. I built Kurage FreqAI Trade because most AI trading systems focus only on prediction. I wanted to build a system that also reviews its mistakes and improves through evidence.
3. Kurage combines FreqAI and LightGBM with AI loops for market analysis, risk control, trade review, and strategy improvement.
4. Every proposed change is tested through backtesting, and a human makes the final decision.
5. It supports both paper trading and live trading. We also publish real performance, losses, incidents, and lessons through a public dashboard and blog. I launched this project to make AI trading more transparent, testable, and continuously improvable. Thank you for reviewing Kurage FreqAI Trade.
